About GreenWave Carwash

GreenWave Carwash was founded to bring professional carcare together with sustainable processes. Our aim: excellent results, reduced environmental impact.

Our mission

Deliver high-quality carwash services using water-reclaim systems, biodegradable cleaning agents, and energy-efficient equipment.

Values

  • Quality - we treat each vehicle with care.
  • Transparency - clear pricing and honest work.
  • Sustainability - every choice considers our planet.

Our team

Trained technicians, certified detailers, and customer-first service staff committed to excellent results.
